The MetaMask Snap

UTL Lives Inside Your Wallet

🦊
kenostod-utl-snap
The UTL MetaMask Snap adds fee redistribution directly into the MetaMask interface. Every user who installs the Snap and transacts generates fees that flow to KENO stakers via the FeeCollector contract. It's invisible to the user — they just transact normally.
