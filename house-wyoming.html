Why Wyoming

The Most Crypto-Forward Banking State

DAO legal recognition — Wyoming was the first US state to legally recognize DAOs. Kenostod's community governance is legally real.
SPDI charter framework — Specifically designed for crypto-native banks. Hold BTC and ETH as bank assets. No equivalent exists elsewhere in the US.
No fractional reserve requirement — SPDI banks must hold 100% of assets. The bank cannot gamble with depositor funds. The Inversion Principle made law.
Cross-border reach — A Wyoming SPDI charter enables Kenostod to serve global unbanked populations with legal US banking infrastructure.
