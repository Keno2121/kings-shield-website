The Number 1729

Why 1,729?

1729 is Ramanujan's Number — the smallest number expressible as the sum of two cubes in two ways: 1³+12³ = 9³+10³ = 1729. Ramanujan was a self-taught mathematical genius born into poverty in India who rewrote the rules of mathematics. He found beauty where others saw nothing. The Queens Chariot NFT carries his story.

1³ + 12³ = 1 + 1728 = 1729
9³ + 10³ = 729 + 1000 = 1729
