The Protocol Stack

01
Solar Power Layer
Ruggedized solar panel charges a deep-cycle battery. The Bunker runs 24/7 regardless of grid availability. Load shedding — South Africa's reality — becomes irrelevant.
02
Offline Blockchain Node
A local blockchain node stores KENO balances, SHIELD transactions, and course completion records offline. When connectivity becomes available, it syncs with the main chains in seconds.
03
B.U.K. Key Management
Every Solar Bunker deployment ships with B.U.K. cards for community members. Physical key management that survives the conditions real people live in.
04
Local Education Node
The 21 Kenostod courses run locally from the Bunker hardware. Students earn KENO completing courses — no internet, no delays, no data cost. The education is there.
05
Mesh Network Resilience
Multiple Bunkers in a region can form a mesh network, syncing with each other before hitting the internet. The Sovereign Economy survives infrastructure failure because it was designed for it from day one.
